What Is Dark Blues Music?

Dark blues music is the blues stripped down to bone and shadow. It doesn't celebrate — it witnesses. It lives in the space between slide guitar notes, in the pause before the vocal drops low, in the stories that don't resolve clean.

From the Delta crossroads where Robert Johnson sold his soul to the modern back roads where dark country meets the blues tradition, dark blues has always been the sound of people who've seen too much and felt it all anyway.

It's not sad music. It's honest music.
